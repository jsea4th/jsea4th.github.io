About Me

Hi! My name is Jamal Seaforth and I am a senior at the University of Maryland pursuing a double degree in Broadcast Journalism and International Relations with a minor in International Development and Conflict Management. I am a resident assitant on campus and I work for the Maryland football team where I am a videographer. I also serve as president for the university's Caribbean Students Association (CSA). My interest are in sports and development in the Caribbean.

Throughout my four years at UMD, working in athletics, student affairs, campus life, and resident life has allowed me to gain a lot of experience during my time at Maryland and I have learned a lot of key skills that are needed in the real world. As I turn to the next chapter in my life, I am looking forward to applying my degrees and skills to become a better leader and make the world a better place.
